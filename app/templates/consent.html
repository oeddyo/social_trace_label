Consent Form

 
Purpose of this Study
The goal of this project is to explore the factors that could influence people’s perception and interaction with tweets.
Experiment Procedure
You will read 12 tweets and rate your impressions about each of them. Then you will fill out a short survey about demographics.
Expected Duration
This experiment will be about 20 minutes.
Participant Requirements
Participants must be at least 18 years old to participate.
Risks
The risks and discomfort associated with participation in this study are no greater than those ordinarily encountered in daily life.
